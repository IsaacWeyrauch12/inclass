The Rimac Nevera (pronounced: [rǐːmat͡s něʋeːra]) is an all-electric sports car designed and manufactured by the Croatian automotive manufacturer Rimac Automobili.[4] The first production prototype car was released in August 2021.[5][6] Rimac will produce 150 vehicles[7][8] and is currently in the process of homologation for the global market.[9] Nevera is manufactured in the same factory and at the same rate (of roughly 1 per week) as the Pininfarina Battista, which is based on the same platform.
